Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Welcome to DSSSB PYQ. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the best mock test experience and maintain global leaderboards, we collect the following information when you register:

  • Personal Identification Data: Your Name and Mobile Number (used exclusively as your unique App ID for progress tracking and PRO upgrades).
  • Performance Data: Your mock test scores, time taken, attempted questions, and overall accuracy to generate analytical reports.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your student account.
  • To track your syllabus progress and daily streaks.
  • To display your ranking on the Global Leaderboard.
  • To facilitate PRO account upgrades via Telegram support.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app utilizes third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These include:

  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and security.
  • Firebase Realtime Database: For secure storage of your test results and profile data.

4. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. Our app is equipped with anti-piracy measures to ensure a secure CBT environment. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact our Admin Team.
